About us

Welcome to Stripes Interiors LLC

The Vision

Our journey begun with a vision:

Truth serves life, so technology, beauty.

Every nation and empire across history thrived, and every social and cultural evolution was initiated, not on money but on that which moves the emotions; on beautiful ideas and ideals--- It is owing to millennia of collaboration to build the most astounding works of beauty that Europe had become the cradle of civilization, is the most visited continent in the world, and is priceless to the global economy.

Why is this so? Because in a world that runs on emotions, ideas influence all---because people don't buy what you do, they buy why you do it; because they don't suffer for money, but for ideas and ideals that they find beautiful. Thus, man does not live by bread alone.

Therefore, by constantly reminding individuals and firms of what they stand for and so who they are, aesthetically beautiful principles help raise civilization to a new level of modernity.

“He alone is modem who is fully conscious of the present” (Jung)

We say ‘modern’ and ‘futuristic’; we mean conscious. We do not waste time trying to imagine what a future would be like; instead, we consciously make it by learning from history and from nature.

We emphasize the past in order to hold the scales against our break with tradition. For us, "today” stands between “yesterday” and "tomorrow”, and forms a link between past and future. After all, the really modern is often to be found among those who call themselves ‘old-fashioned’.

Our Craft

In nature, there are no straight lines.

When it comes to what things look like, the Western world and modern design is obsessed by perfection, by symmetry and ideal proportion, by invincibility, by stability, by youth, flawlessness… while the aesthetics of the Orient languish.

Our design philosophy is to harmonize this imbalance and to transcend the limits of expression to allow our clients to most fully express their brand principles.

“It is only shallow people who do not judge by appearances." - Oscar Wilde

Our Services

We offer

displays. banners. graphics. flooring. platforms. stands. prints.

It all starts with you.

On each project we listen, we immerse ourselves in your business and the challenges you face to determine the functional and aesthetic needs of the project and to assimilate your expectations. We then provide comprehensive and complete design beginning with an initial design concept, through installation and detailing of final design elements to completion

Our expertise lay in design solutions that are creative and innovative yet accountable and whole, and that have the power to enlighten. Our practicality is beauty, and our beauty, practical.

We provide a wide range of services including superior quality display stands, hanging banners, vinyl graphics and flooring, raised platforms etc. We provide all services related to Exhibition Stand Designs, Signages and Interiors. Fully in-house, we are equipped with state of the art manufacturing and printing set up, we’re the one-stop-shop when it comes to your exhibition needs and requirements.
